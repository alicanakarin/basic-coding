Magnolia Recipe

Magnolia, a milk-based dessert of American origin, is made primarily with pudding, biscuits, and fruit. It has gained popularity in many countries, especially in its strawberry version. Unlike traditional pudding recipes, it is prepared by adding raw cream.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 packet vanilla
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons starch (corn or wheat starch)
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1.5 tea glasses cream

For the layers of the Magnolia dessert:

  • 1 packet baby biscuits
  • 15–20 strawberries

Instructions

  1. In a pot, add the milk, sugar, starch, flour, and egg yolk. Mix well.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it reaches a pudding-like consistency.
  3. Remove from the heat, add the vanilla, and stir. Once it starts to cool, add the cream and continue mixing.
  4. Meanwhile, crush the biscuits in a food processor. Remove the stems of the strawberries and cut them into your desired sizes.
  5. Take a few spoonfuls of the cooled pudding and spread it on the bottom of a serving cup.
  6. Add a layer of biscuits and strawberries on top.
  7. Repeat the layers until all the biscuits, pudding, and strawberries are used up, dividing them evenly among the cups.
  8. Refrigerate for 3–4 hours to chill. At the time of serving, decorate the top with biscuits, strawberries, bananas, chocolate sauce, or other toppings of your choice.
  9. Your Magnolia dessert is ready. Enjoy!
